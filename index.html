System Overview

This is not a collection of separate tools. Dynamic Code will design and build one integrated platform where every part of Trade Coast Hotel's operations connects — hotel, bistro, shuttle, and guest management — all accessible from a single, role-based admin portal.

🏨
Hotel Booking Management
Room availability, booking requests, confirmations, guest profiles, and card tokenisation for damage coverage. Admin can manage all bookings, modify dates, and process charges.
🍽️
Online Ordering Management
Full menu management with item toggling, pricing, categories, and descriptions. Orders flow directly to the backend. Admin can process billing, refunds, and partial refunds.
📋
Table Booking Management
Guest-facing table reservation system with date, time, and party size selection. Email confirmations sent automatically on request and confirmation.
🚌
Shuttle Booking Management
Scheduled shuttle runs with seat-based capacity. Guests book seats on specific runs. Admin manages schedules, capacity, and passenger lists with real-time visibility.
👥
Contact Management (CRM)
Centralised guest profiles built automatically from bookings and orders. Full history of stays, orders, and shuttle usage — enabling personalised communication and follow-up.
🌐
Brand New Website
A modern, mobile-responsive website for Trade Coast Hotel — beautifully designed and fully integrated with all booking and ordering modules. Staging: tradecoasthotel.com, Production: tradecoasthotel.com.au

One Platform. Every Touchpoint.

  • All modules share a single database — guest data, bookings, and orders are connected
  • Email confirmations sent automatically: Request Received and Booking Confirmed
  • Card tokenisation for hotel guests: charge for damages or future orders without re-entering card details
  • Role-based team access: staff see only what they need, nothing more

Module Detail

Hotel Booking Management

  • Room availability calendar with real-time capacity management
  • Guest booking flow: room selection, dates, guest details, card tokenisation
  • Automated email confirmations: Request Received and Booking Confirmed
  • Card tokenisation: hotel can charge for damages or add-ons post-stay without re-entering card details
  • Admin: view, modify, cancel, and manage all hotel bookings from one dashboard

Online Ordering Management

  • Guest-facing menu with categories, item descriptions, pricing, and images
  • Admin can add, edit, remove menu items and toggle availability on/off in real time
  • Orders processed and routed to admin dashboard with live notifications
  • Full billing management: charge, refund, and partial refund capability
  • Card tokenisation: returning guests can reorder without re-entering payment details

Table Booking Management

  • Guest-facing reservation form: date, time, party size, and contact details
  • Admin manages seating capacity and confirms or adjusts reservations
  • Automated email: Request Received on submission, Booking Confirmed on approval
  • Table availability managed in the admin portal with a clear calendar view

Shuttle Booking Management

  • Admin creates scheduled shuttle runs with fixed routes, times, and seat capacity
  • Guests select available runs and confirm their seat — no overbooking
  • Automated booking confirmations via email
  • Admin views passenger manifests per run with real-time seat availability

Contact Management (CRM)

  • Guest profiles created automatically from any booking or order
  • Full activity history: hotel stays, table bookings, food orders, shuttle trips
  • Enables personalised follow-up, loyalty communications, and guest insights
  • Searchable and exportable contact database for the team

Admin Portal &
Team Permissions

The admin portal is the operational heart of the system. Every team member logs in to one place — but each person sees only what is relevant to their role.

Role-Based Access Control

🔑
Full Access (Owner / Manager)
Complete visibility and control across all modules: hotel, bistro, shuttle, CRM, billing, and reporting. User management and permission assignment.
🏨
Hotel Staff
Access to hotel bookings, guest check-ins, room management, and card charge/refund functionality. No access to bistro or shuttle modules.
🍽️
Bistro Staff
Access to online orders, table bookings, menu management, and order billing. No access to hotel or shuttle modules.
🚌
Shuttle Coordinator
Access to shuttle run schedules, passenger bookings, and capacity management only.

Admin Portal Features

  • Single login, role-filtered dashboard for every team member
  • Add, edit, and deactivate team members with specific permission sets
  • Full audit trail: see who changed what and when across the system
  • Notification centre for new bookings, orders, and alerts
  • Reporting across all modules: occupancy, revenue, covers, and shuttle utilisation

Website Integration

  • Brand new modern website built and fully integrated with all booking modules
  • Guests can book rooms, order food, reserve tables, and book shuttle from the website
  • Mobile-optimised and fast — built for the modern hospitality guest
  • Staging environment: tradecoasthotel.com
  • Production launch: tradecoasthotel.com.au
  • Backend admin login: admin@tradecoasthotel.com.au
